How to Buy ALTX

  1. Connect your wallet (MetaMask, Rabby, etc.).

  3. Choose V2 (legacy swap).
  4. Select USDC as “From”.
  5. Paste ALTX contract: 0x248E5dc032867EEaeaACaCe284Ce988c144F3c5A.
